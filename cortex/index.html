The nerve center of your digital life

Cortex stores your digital content in one place and organize it into tasks. Welcome to the new digital workspace.

Focus is key to getting things done

Cortex removes all distractions so you can fix in on one thing at a time. Want to do something? Create a task and everything you do will be grouped together, one file at a time.

Connections power productivity

Zoom out and see the bigger picture. Cortex mindmaps your content so you can see how files relate to one another. Let the content store itself and never forget a thing.

Think outside of the task at hand

Working on something, but want to save an idea for later or perform a quick task? Quick Access serves as a thought bubble that lets you store thought and perform quick tasks, so you don't forget a thing.
