Get Involved

At Stecknologies, we have been working to create software that feels more personalized and less manufactured. With Opus Mage, we want to create a new home for the user's life by reworking the framework of the internet from the ground up. If we rely on the internet so much in our daily lives, why can't it be smarter, safer, and more personalized? In building a project as ambitious and exciting as Opus Mage, it is important to have a diverse group of creative, hard-working, and motivated individuals working to bring this product to life. No matter whether you are a developer, designer, project manager, thinker, or not even into technology, we encourage you to join the community and start engaging in the project. We need your help to bring this product to life.


Early Roadmap

Once a community is assembled, we will work together to create the basic tech stack. Most likely, it will be creating a model for a user profile that connects all of the user's online information and can be used as a single-sign-on mechanism. The end goal is to create a framework that a) revolves around the user's profile, b) includes an array of smart interface features, and c) makes it easier to create and share information.
